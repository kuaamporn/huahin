Employee Roles

Assign multiple roles per employee to control access:
  • owner: Full access, view finance, and can edit verified transactions.
  • frontdesk: Can add and manage rooms bookings.
  • technician: Can view room status and resolve maintenance tasks.
  • housekeeping / housekeeping_precious / housekeeping_moment: Can view room status and mark rooms as cleaned. (All, Precious only, or Moment only).
  • revenue: Can add revenue records.
  • expense: Can add expense records.
  • accountant: Can view finance summaries and lists.
